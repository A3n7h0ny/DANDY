Who We Are

Dandy Hair is a full-service beauty studio based at Shop 108 in Sandton, Johannesburg. We are the responsible party for all personal information collected through this website and during bookings.

Address: Shop 108, Sandton, Johannesburg, 2191  ·  Phone: 067 653 1696

Information We Collect

When you contact us or book an appointment, we may collect:

  • Contact details: Name, phone number, email address
  • Booking information: Service requested, appointment details
  • Website usage: IP address, browser type, pages visited
  • Communications: Messages sent via our contact form, WhatsApp or Instagram DM

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used only for the following purposes:

  • To confirm and manage your salon appointments
  • To contact you about your booking or service
  • To send appointment reminders if requested
  • To improve our services and website
  • To comply with legal obligations under South African law

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security & Retention

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access or misuse. Client information is retained for up to 3 years for booking records and legal compliance, after which it is securely deleted.

To request deletion of your personal information at any time, contact us at 067 653 1696 or visit us at Shop 108, Sandton, Johannesburg.

Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies required for the site to function. We do not use tracking, advertising or third-party analytics cookies. By continuing to use our website you consent to essential cookies only.
